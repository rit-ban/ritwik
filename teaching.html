Mentoring

Over the years, several students have worked with me as Research Associates. After their RA stint, they have gone on to join excellent PhD programs. I quite enjoy mentoring them as they transition from Masters programs to PhD programs — in the process, they get to wet their feet in economics research and I get to learn a lot.

  • Priyoma Mustafi 2017–2019  ·  University of Pittsburgh
